MergeMap
  • {{item}}
    • MergeMap subscribes to two streams, creating a new stream. You can then subsribe to that final stream. In this case, we're using one observable that produces numbers, and a second one that produces strings in the form of US states. We create a final stream that joins those two together. We subscribe to it and then push it into an array. We call observer.complete() on each of the Observables so that they end after a certain number of cycles.
